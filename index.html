Welcome

This website has been developed to explain the proposed development of Marcris House, Coopersale Lane, Theydon Bois and invite comments from neighbours and other interested parties before a full planning application is submitted.

About the Development

The development will comprise of demolition of the existing building and construction of 14 new two bedroom luxury apartments in a parkland setting. The building has the highest standard of vernacular architecture and will use the best materials.

The site layout has been determined by careful analysis of the existing site and surrounding areas.

The existing property is not visible from the road but the new design appears as an original grand house to the centre with extensions on both sides. The retained entrance and new exist to the site make access and egress safer and reduce noise and light pollution to the adjacent property.

Parking is uncovered and will blend into the existing landscape. Paths extend through the site and the main areas of amenity are to the south and west. High quality and dense copse of trees are retained to the front of the site to soften the new buildings.

Code 4 Sustainable and Lifetime Homes standards will be adopted to create a truly sustainable building.

Key Facts

  • 3 floors
  • 11 x 2 bed apartments
  • 22 parking spaces
  • Highly landscaped grounds with retention of existing trees and new planting
  • Views over open countryside
